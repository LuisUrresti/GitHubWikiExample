Initial Considerations and Approach

When working with Azure, the organizational and hierarchical structure of the elements could be, like for the rest of the Cloud providers, different for each company to accommodate better their preferred way of working and managing IT resources. Following requirements were considered for defining Iberdrola’s landing zone.
  • Define TO-BE subscriptions model based in Iberdrola’s organization.
  • Define specific criteria about when and where create a specific subscription for a use case.
  • Include Management groups and their capabilities of simplifying governance of subscriptions and resources.
  • Ensure that the model suits whit actual and future needs, specially taking into account a massive grow in coming years.
  • Keep in mind isolation between resources for different concerns.
  • Describe networking model within a subscription for standard use cases.
  • Resources cannot be accessed directly from internet.
  • All subscription subnets should be accessed from other subscriptions subnets as well as from on premises.
